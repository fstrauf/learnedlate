## 5. Engagement Tactics

### 5.1 Comment Strategy

Target these types of posts to comment on (with substantive, non-promotional takes):
- AI adoption/failure discussions
- NZ/AU business technology posts
- CTO/CIO content about digital transformation
- Government technology and innovation announcements
- Posts from potential referral partners (management consultants, accountants, lawyers)

### 5.2 DM Follow-Up Framework

When someone engages meaningfully with your content:

1. **Thank + add value** — "Thanks for the comment — you raised a good point about [X]. I actually wrote about that here: [link]"
2. **Ask, don't pitch** — "Curious — are you seeing this in your business too?"
3. **Offer, don't sell** — "Happy to do a quick readiness check if you're exploring this. No cost, no obligation."
